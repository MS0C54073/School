Gideon University is a new medical university aimed at breaking new frontiers in higher education

Gideon University is the one of the best medical universities in Zambia. We offer a wide range of medical health programmes. We enroll students twice a year (January and June) for both our diploma and degree programmes. We have support in place for our students to graduate on time as well-rounded, responsible citizens fully prepared to provide high quality health services in the Zambian health sector.

Our Vision and Mission

Breaking new frontiers in higher education. Gideon Robert University aims at raising the bar in medical training in Zambia. With our state of the art equipment, we strive to produce competent health practioners whose works will speak highly of their institution of training.

Programmes Offered

  4. Dental Therapy

Clubs and Sports Activities

St. Monica's Girls Secondary School has the following clubs for pupils;

  • Anti-AIDS club: This sensitizes pupils on sexual health matters. Specialized health educators are regularly invited to teach children about sexual health.
  • Debate club: This club aims at developing our pupils interest and self-esteem so that they grow into good debators on issues of relevance
  • Sports Activities: The sports pitch below hosts different sports activities ranging from football, Athletics to high jump, long jump. The school holds a sports event every year to celebrate the sports talent of our pupils
