St. Monica's Girls Secondary School is amongst the best schools in Eastern province.

St. Monica's Girls Secondary School has the best modern learning infrastructure and materials thus ensuring that our students receive the best quality education and realize their dreams. Our teachers are the best and deliver the best teaching services to establish a firm educational foundation to pupils. This implies that bringing your child to St. Monica's Girls Secondary School is going to be the best education decision you will make for your child.

Our Vision and Mission

St. Monica's Girls Secondary School exists to establish strong education foundations for all our pupils. We strive to impart sound principles and values on our learners so that they grow into responsible intellectual members of society.

Meet our teachers

The following are the three best teachers at our school with a brief description of them;

Walter White

Head Teacher

Meet our Head teacher who hepls bring out the best in both teachers and pupils. He has served as the schools head teacher since 2017 and during his time, the grade 9 and 12 pass rate has gradually improved to staggering heights of 90% and 92% respectively.

Bwalya Musonda

Math Teacher

He has led the school to a Math awards competition victory at the recent Inter-school competition. Mr Musonda has been teaching Math for over 20 years and helps bring out the best in his students through his simplistic method of teaching math.

Giogina Mwelwa

Hygiene Mentor

She is our girls Hygiene mentor and helps educate our pupils on personal hygiene practices through weekly engagements with all pupils. She is also an excellent Physics teacher.

Clubs and Sports Activities

St. Monica's Girls Secondary School has the following clubs for pupils;

  • Anti-AIDS club: This sensitizes pupils on sexual health matters. Specialized health educators are regularly invited to teach children about sexual health.
  • Debate club: This club aims at developing our pupils interest and self-esteem so that they grow into good debators on issues of relevance
  • Sports Activities: The sports pitch below hosts different sports activities ranging from football, Athletics to high jump, long jump. The school holds a sports event every year to celebrate the sports talent of our pupils
