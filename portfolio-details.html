The University of Zambia is the biggest and most reputable university in Zambia.

A very broad range of programmes are offered at UNZA. Located along Great North road, UNZA main campus has over 5,000 enrollments every academic year and over 10,000 graduates every year. According to the 2021 tertiary institutions ratings by zema, it was ranked number 1. With its state of the art infrastructure and the best lecturers in the country, UNZA is the best institution to train for your professional career.

Programmes Offered

The University of Zambia main campus has 3 main schools listed below with their respective programmes offered under each school;

  1. School of Education
  2. School of Humanities and Social Sciences
  3. School of Natural Sciences
